Available Voice Commands

Entertainment and Communication:

  • youtube - open YouTube/If you say something after the word "YouTube", then it will be requested in YouTube
  • twitch - open Twitch/If you say something after the word "Twitch", then it will be requested in Twitch
  • steam - open Steam
  • discord - open Discord
  • telegram - open Telegram
  • music - open Yandex Music

Information:

  • date - find out the date
  • time - find out the time
  • weather - find out the weather
  • translate - open translator

Fun Commands:

  • hello - greeting
  • goodbye - closure

Search Engines:

  • google - If you say something after the word "Google", then it will be requested in Google
  • yandex - If you say something after the word "Yandex", then it will be requested in Yandex
Output will be displayed here.
